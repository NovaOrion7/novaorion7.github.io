🌤️ Blue Air - Weather App

Modern and user-friendly weather app. Real-time weather information, 5-day forecast, and fun mini game!

Features

🌍 Multi-language Support

Available in Turkish, English, Japanese, German, and Portuguese

🎨 Theme Selection

Choose between Light, Dark, or Automatic themes based on your preference

📍 GPS Location Support

Automatically detect your location for accurate weather information

🔍 City Search

Search for weather information in any city around the world

⭐ Save Favorite Cities

Save your frequently visited cities for quick access

⏱️ Detailed Forecasts

3-hourly updates and 5-day weather predictions

🌦️ Animated Weather Icons

Lottie animations for beautiful and intuitive weather representations

☔ Umbrella Rain Catch Game

Fun mini-game to enjoy while checking the weather

🔔 Background Notifications

Receive important weather alerts even when the app is closed

📱 Modern Design

Clean edge-to-edge interface with intuitive navigation
